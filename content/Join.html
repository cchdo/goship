Join the GO-SHIP Email List

The GO-SHIP email list is used to provide news and announcements to the repeat hydrographic community. There are currently 164 subscribers.

To subscribe to the GO-SHIP email list, send an email to sympa@lists.unesco.org with "subscribe go-ship" in the subject line. 

To unsubscribe from this list, send an email to sympa@lists.unesco.org  with "unsubscribe go-ship" in the subject line.
